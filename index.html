All Things Glam

Green Glamour is a sustainable beauty app that makes it easier for consumers to make ethical and sustainable choices when it comes to their beauty products.

  • 89% of the over 10,500 ingredients used in personal care products have not been evaluated for safety. Not only can beauty products be unsafe for your skin, but they also harm the environment. These chemicals have contributed to deforestation and have been found in agricultural soil, propelling an already rapid rate of climate change.
  • We believe that beauty should not come at the cost of our planet, people, or animals, and our mission is to promote sustainable and inclusive beauty practices.
